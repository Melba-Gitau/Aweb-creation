Services.

Livsus Geoservices endeavors to provide a wide range of consultancy services in Land Survey field.
Specifically:
Demarcation surveys

We offer layout marking and demarcation, inclusive of roads, plots, commercial space including fencing.

Title surveys

Accurate determination of property extends and size.
Processing registration and transfers of titles to properties, undertake searches for due diligence and for registration of encumbrances by financial institutions.

Topography Surveys.

We determine changes in elevation on a parcel of land, providing us with a wealth of information regarding the property at hand, resulting in; Boundary Establishment Maps, Topographical Maps, Design Survey Maps, Architectural Survey Maps,Slope analysis Maps, As-built Surveys among others.

GI.System

Remote-based Mapping

We carry out land use land cover mapping at large scale to facilitate planning and monitoring of climate resilience and climate change impact.

Mapping surveys

We professionally determine the positions of points on the surface of the earth through measuring the distances, directions, angles, and elevations between them.

Sectional Property Surveys.

We undertake sectional properties surveys that entail accurate measurements of units in apartments and drawing of sectional plans for tilting of units according to Sectional properties Act 2020.

Land sales and marketting.

Through complex measures, we find tenants to sell real property, implementing strategic and innovative ideas that gain an advantage over the competition..

Property Succession
